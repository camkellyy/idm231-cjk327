How to Find Your Pantone Zodiac!

Pantone is a color matching system widely used by artists and designers of all kinds. Pantone chooses a color annually to be named Pantone Color of the Year! Want to know what color you are based on your birthday? Well then you're in the right place!

By typing in or selecting your birthdate and clicking the submit button, your personal Pantone Zodiac will appear! You can also explore what each Pantone Color of the Year is in relation to the Zodiac Signs by clicking the arrow buttons on either side of the color! Happy color matching!
